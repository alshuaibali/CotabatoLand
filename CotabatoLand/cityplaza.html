COTABATO CITY PLAZA

We love COTABATO CITY

The plaza lies at the very heart of the busy city, along a major thoroughfare, and surrounded by establishments and business centers. This is a landmark where locals meet, congregate or maybe just to pass time during day time.

But as the sun goes down and food stands, fruit carts, and mobile coffee shops come rolling down the busy center, and the place becomes livelier. Spare well-known restaurants for a day, and grab a barbeque and pastil from one of the numerous stands for a cheap but delicious dinner.
